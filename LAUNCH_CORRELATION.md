# SpaceApp Launch Correlation Engine

## Purpose

The Launch Correlation Engine identifies potential launch
activities by correlating regulatory, aviation, maritime,
and mission intelligence signals.

---

## NOTAM Processing

NOTAM events provide aviation restrictions that may
indicate launch preparation activity.

Event:

NOTAMEvent.json

Schema:

schemas/NOTAMEvent.schema.json

---

## NTM Processing

NTM events provide maritime restrictions and notices
that may indicate launch activity.

Event:

NTMEvent.json

Schema:

schemas/NTMEvent.schema.json

---

## Regulatory Intelligence

Sources may include:

- FAA
- USCG
- International regulators
- Launch operators

---

## Launch Detection

Launch detection combines:

- NOTAM signals
- NTM signals
- Mission intelligence
- Historical launch patterns

---

## Correlation Scoring

Confidence levels:

MEDIUM

HIGH

Launch Correlation Engine.
